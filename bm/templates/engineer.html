Agriculture Engineering

Image

Agricultural engineering is the engineering agricultural production and processing. Agricultural engineering combines the disciplines of mechanical, civil, electrical, Food science and chemical engineering principles with a knowledge of agricultural principles according to technological principles.

Why agricultural engineering is important?

Agricultural engineers typically do the following: Design agricultural machinery components and equipment, using computer-aided design (CAD) technology. Test agricultural machinery and equipment to ensure that they perform properly. Design food-processing plants and supervise manufacturing operations.
